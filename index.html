What We Do

Multiple verticals. One relentless focus: results.

Miami Metro combines boots-on-the-ground field sales teams with AI-driven marketing infrastructure — built to generate real revenue, not just impressions.

Telecommunications

Authorized ISO for Assurance Wireless / T-Mobile. Our field agents connect underserved communities across South Florida with free government-subsidized wireless plans through the Lifeline program — door to door, event-based, and community outreach.

AI-Powered Marketing

Not your typical marketing agency. We build content engines, automate lead generation, and use AI to produce high-converting assets at scale — across social media, video, and digital channels. Traffic and sales, not just reach.

Content Creation

From scripted short-form video to branded social campaigns, we produce content that moves audiences and drives measurable action. Built for the algorithm, designed for real conversion — not just likes.

Field Sales Operations

We build, train, and deploy high-performance field sales teams across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach. Our agents show up, connect with real people, and close — at the door, at events, and in the community.
